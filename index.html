About

I am Keivan Rahmani, a Nanoengineering Ph.D. student at the University of California San Diego, conducting research in Professor Zeinab Jahed's Bio-Nano-Electronics (Bione) Lab. I am interested in the intersection of AI, Nanotechnology, and biological systems. Currently, I am working on label-free methods to detect intracellular action potential and rapid hiPSC-CM (human induced pluripotent stem cell-derived cardiomyocytes) parameters estimation using RL (Reinforcement Learning) based voltage protocol.
